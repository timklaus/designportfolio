Royal Rumble 2020

The Royal Rumble is an annual pinball tournament put on by the Portland PDX Pinball Leauge. For the 2020 tournament I was asked to create an original poster graphic inspired by the 90's era WWF Royal Rumble Logo. I created two colorways for the posters, both of which were printed and posted to promote the event. The graphic I created was also used to for t-shirts to commemorate the event.
